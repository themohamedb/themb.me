---
title: From idea to working product
description: How I move from rough thought to interface, workflow, and shipped result.
date: "2026-06-22"
---

Most ideas die in the gap between thinking and building. I try to close that gap quickly.

It usually starts with a rough note in Notion. Then a mobile-first interface in Figma. Then a small working version in code — not perfect, but real enough to test.

I ship small, review honestly, and improve the next version. The first version teaches me what the second version should be.

This is how I move from rough thought to interface, workflow, and something people can actually use.
